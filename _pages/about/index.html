I am Jiwei Sun(孙稷威), a PhD researcher in Faculty of Education at University of Macau. My research focuses on computational thinking (CT), collaborative learning, and science/STEM education in K–12 education. My main research work explored how to help students learning computational thinking skills in K-12 education.

Research Interests

  • Computational thinking
  • Collaborative learning
  • Educational technology
  • Science/STEM education

Prefered Methods

My research uses mixed methods, including qualitative content analysis, cluster analysis, lag sequential analysis, epistemic network analysis, SEM, and machine learning. I usually conduct data analysis using Python, R, and SPSS. I welcome collaborations that use these methods, as well as other relevant methodological approaches.
